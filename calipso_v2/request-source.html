Requesting A Source

Your requests matter!

A new source request means that you want your data from a company that is not currently a Calipso compliant partner. By requesting a source you acknowledge that once this company becomes a source your request will be automatically fulfilled. Please refer to our terms and conditions for more details about this feature.

Requesting a new source is important because it allows the Calipso AI to understand where your data may reside. It is our goal to return all data to your hands for your control and by requesting a source you are taking part in a valuable part of the process.
