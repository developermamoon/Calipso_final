Why Consumer Info?

Consumer information is general information that increases the accuracy of your data pull. This information includes commonly used apps, websites, and stores; as well as general questions about media consumption. By answering these questions you increase the chances of finding all of the data involved in your personal corner of the IoT. All questions here are optional, but are very important to creating an accurate Ai for you. Once your Ai has been generated you will receive more options to further customize your consumer information.
